Version 2.0

1. Linking

Aliquoted cryovials are linked with their respective EDTA tube after centrifuge and separation.

2. Cryovial Packing

Aliquoted cryovials are placed in cryovial boxes, by distributing the samples to different boxes.

3. Storage

Cryovial boxes are placed in the freezer.

4. Synchronize

Newly collected data is sent to the server.
